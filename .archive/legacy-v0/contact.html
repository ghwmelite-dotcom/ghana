Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my application?

You can track any government service application by visiting the relevant service page and entering your application reference number in the tracking section.

What are the office hours?

Most government offices are open Monday to Thursday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Friday from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Offices are closed on weekends and public holidays.

How long does it take to get a response?

For email inquiries, we aim to respond within 24-48 business hours. Phone and live chat support provides immediate assistance during office hours.

Can I submit documents online?

Yes, many services now accept online document submissions. Visit the specific service page to see if online submission is available for your needs.
