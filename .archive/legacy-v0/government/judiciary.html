Functions of the Judiciary

The vital roles the judiciary plays in Ghana's democracy

📖

Constitutional Interpretation

The Supreme Court has exclusive authority to interpret the Constitution, ensuring all laws and government actions comply with the supreme law of the land.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution

Courts resolve civil disputes between individuals, organizations, and government entities through fair and impartial adjudication based on law.

🛡️

Protection of Rights

The judiciary safeguards fundamental human rights and freedoms guaranteed by the Constitution, providing remedies when rights are violated.

🔍

Judicial Review

Courts review the constitutionality of legislation and executive actions, serving as a check on the other branches of government.

⚔️

Criminal Justice

The courts try criminal cases, determine guilt or innocence, and impose appropriate sentences to maintain law and order in society.

📝

Legal Precedent

Higher court decisions create binding precedents that guide lower courts, ensuring consistency and predictability in the application of law.

The Chief Justice

Head of the Judiciary

The Chief Justice is the head of the Judiciary and presides over the Supreme Court. Appointed by the President in consultation with the Council of State and approved by Parliament, the Chief Justice is responsible for the administration of the Judicial Service and ensuring the efficient delivery of justice throughout Ghana.

Access to Justice

Services and initiatives to ensure justice is accessible to all Ghanaians

💼

Legal Aid

Free legal representation for persons who cannot afford lawyers, ensuring equal access to justice regardless of financial status.

🏘️

Community Courts

Local tribunals and alternative dispute resolution mechanisms that bring justice closer to communities.

💻

E-Justice

Digital court services including electronic filing, virtual hearings, and online case tracking for convenience.

📢

Public Education

Programs to educate citizens about their rights, court procedures, and how to access judicial services.

🤝

ADR Center

Alternative Dispute Resolution services including mediation and arbitration for faster, cost-effective resolution.

♿

Accessibility

Initiatives to make courts accessible to persons with disabilities and vulnerable groups.
