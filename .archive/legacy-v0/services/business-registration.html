Types of Business Registration

Select the business structure that best suits your needs.

Sole Proprietorship

Single-owner business with simplest registration process. Owner has unlimited liability for business debts.

Registration Fee GHS 50
Processing Time 1-3 Days

Partnership

Two or more individuals sharing profits, losses, and management responsibilities. Requires partnership agreement.

Registration Fee GHS 150
Processing Time 3-5 Days

Private Limited Company

Separate legal entity with limited liability. Shares are not publicly traded. Requires at least one shareholder and one director.

Registration Fee GHS 380+
Processing Time 5-10 Days

External Company

Foreign company establishing a branch or place of business in Ghana. Must register within 28 days of establishment.

Registration Fee GHS 2,500+
Processing Time 10-15 Days
What You Need

Registration Requirements

For Individuals (Sole Proprietorship)

  • Ghana Card or valid national ID
  • TIN (Tax Identification Number)
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Business name (3 preferred names)
  • Business address details

For Companies (Limited Liability)

  • Company Regulations (Form 4)
  • Ghana Card of all directors/shareholders
  • TIN for all directors/shareholders
  • Registered office address
  • Stated capital (min GHS 500 for private)

Registration Process

Follow these steps to register your business in Ghana.

1

Name Search

Search and reserve your business name online through the RGD portal. Submit 3 preferred names in order of priority.

2

Complete Application

Fill out the online registration form with company details, directors, shareholders, and registered address information.

3

Upload Documents

Upload required documents including ID cards, TIN certificates, and company regulations (for limited companies).

4

Pay Fees

Pay registration fees online via mobile money, bank transfer, or at any RGD office.

5

Receive Certificate

Upon approval, download your certificate of registration or collect from RGD office.
