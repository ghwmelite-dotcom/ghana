License Classes

License Categories

Choose the right license category based on the vehicles you intend to drive.

A

Motorcycle

Two-wheeled motor vehicles including motorcycles, scooters, and tricycles.

Motorcycles Scooters Motor Tricycles
B

Light Vehicle

Private cars and light vehicles with maximum weight not exceeding 3,500 kg.

Private Cars SUVs Minibuses (9 seats)
C

Medium Vehicle

Goods vehicles and trucks with weight between 3,500 kg and 7,500 kg.

Light Trucks Delivery Vans Mini Buses
D

Heavy Vehicle

Heavy goods vehicles and buses exceeding 7,500 kg maximum weight.

Trucks Articulated Vehicles Large Buses
E

Articulated

Articulated trailers and heavy combination vehicles.

Trailer Trucks Tankers Container Vehicles
F

Special Permit

Agricultural tractors and other special purpose vehicles.

Tractors Construction Special Equipment
What You Need

Requirements

New License Application

  • Ghana Card (National ID)
  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • Driving school certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs (2)

License Renewal

  • Existing driver's license
  • Ghana Card (National ID)
  • Medical fitness certificate (if over 50)
  • Renewal fee payment
How It Works

Application Process

1

2

3

4
