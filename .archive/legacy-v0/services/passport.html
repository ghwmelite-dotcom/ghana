Requirements

Ensure you have all required documents before starting your application to avoid delays.

  • 1
    Ghana Card (National ID)
    Valid Ghana Card is mandatory for all passport applications
  • 2
    Birth Certificate
    Original birth certificate issued by Births & Deaths Registry
  • 3
    Passport Photos
    4 recent passport-size photos (white background, 35mm x 45mm)
  • 4
    Proof of Ghanaian Citizenship
    For naturalized citizens: Certificate of Naturalization or Registration
  • 5
    Old Passport (for renewals)
    Previous passport for renewal applications

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your passport application.

1
Create Account & Apply Online
Visit the passport portal, create an account, and fill out the application form with your personal details.
2
Upload Documents
Upload scanned copies of your Ghana Card, birth certificate, and passport photo.
3
Pay Application Fee
Make payment online via mobile money, bank transfer, or card payment.
4
Schedule Appointment
Book an appointment at your preferred passport application center for biometric capture.
5
Visit Center for Biometrics
Attend your appointment with original documents for verification and biometric capture.
6
Collect Passport
Receive notification when your passport is ready for collection or home delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, you can start your passport application online through the official Ghana Passport Application Portal. You'll need to create an account, fill out the application form, upload documents, and pay the fee online. However, you must visit a passport application center in person for biometric capture.
Standard processing takes 5-7 business days after biometric capture. Express processing is available for 24-48 hours at an additional fee. Processing times may vary during peak periods.
For a child's passport, you'll need: Child's birth certificate, Child's Ghana Card (if 6 years or older), Parent/Guardian's Ghana Card, Parent/Guardian's passport, Consent letter from both parents (if applicable), and 4 passport photos of the child.
Yes, you can renew your passport up to 12 months before it expires. We recommend renewing at least 6 months before expiry, as many countries require your passport to be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
If your passport is lost or stolen, you should: 1) Report to the nearest police station and obtain a police report, 2) Report the loss to the Passport Office, 3) Apply for a replacement passport with the police report, an affidavit, and standard requirements. If lost abroad, contact the nearest Ghana Embassy or Consulate.
