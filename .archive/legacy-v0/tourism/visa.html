What You Need

Visa Requirements

Gather these documents before starting your application.

Required Documents

  • Valid Passport Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond travel date with 2 blank pages
  • Passport Photo Recent passport-sized photograph with white background (35mm x 45mm)
  • Flight Itinerary Confirmed round-trip flight reservation showing entry and exit dates
  • Accommodation Proof Hotel reservation or invitation letter from host in Ghana
  • Financial Evidence Bank statement or proof of sufficient funds for the duration of stay
  • Yellow Fever Certificate International Certificate of Vaccination for Yellow Fever (mandatory)

Step by Step

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to obtain your Ghana visa.

1

Create an Account

Register on the Ghana Immigration Service e-visa portal with your email address and create a secure password.

2

Complete Application Form

Fill in your personal details, travel information, and purpose of visit. Ensure all information matches your passport.

3

Upload Documents

Upload clear scans of your passport, photo, flight itinerary, accommodation proof, and other required documents.

4

Pay Visa Fee

Make secure payment using credit/debit card, PayPal, or mobile money. You'll receive a payment confirmation.

5

Receive Your Visa

Once approved, download your e-visa and print it. Present it along with your passport upon arrival in Ghana.
