## What we do

The Ministry of Communications and Digitalisation is building Digital Ghana.
NITA manages the government's digital infrastructure and the Ghana.GOV
platform. The National Communications Authority licenses and regulates
telecoms operators so you get better mobile and broadband coverage.
The Cyber Security Authority protects government systems and citizens online
under Act 1038. The Data Protection Commission enforces your rights under
the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843). We are expanding fibre broadband
and rolling out digital public services so every Ghanaian can transact
with government from any device.
