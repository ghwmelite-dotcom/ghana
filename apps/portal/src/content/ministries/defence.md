## What we do

The Ministry of Defence keeps Ghana safe. We direct the Ghana Armed Forces —
the Army, Navy, and Air Force — in defending our land, sea, and airspace.
We contribute troops to United Nations and African Union peacekeeping missions
worldwide. We also support civilian authorities during natural disasters and
public-health emergencies. Our Defence Intelligence unit monitors threats to
national security so Ghana stays one step ahead.
