## What we do

The Ministry of Education makes sure every child in Ghana can learn. We set
the national curriculum through NaCCA, manage over 20,000 schools through the
Ghana Education Service, and oversee universities through the Ghana Tertiary
Education Commission. The National Service Secretariat posts graduates to
public institutions for their mandatory national service year. Our goal is
a well-educated, skilled Ghana ready to compete globally.
