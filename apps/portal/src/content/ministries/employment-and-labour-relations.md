## What we do

The Ministry of Employment and Labour Relations protects workers and builds a
thriving job market. SSNIT collects pension contributions and pays retirement
and invalidity benefits to millions of Ghanaian workers. The Fair Wages and
Salaries Commission ensures public-sector workers are paid fairly. The Labour
Commission mediates disputes between employers and employees so conflicts are
resolved without strikes. We also work to formalise the informal economy so more
workers access pensions, health insurance, and legal protections.
