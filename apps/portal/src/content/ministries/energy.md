## What we do

The Ministry of Energy powers Ghana. We oversee the generation, transmission,
and distribution of electricity across all sixteen regions. GNPC manages Ghana's
oil and gas resources offshore. The Energy Commission licenses energy providers
and sets efficiency standards. We are expanding solar and other renewables to
reduce the cost of power and reach communities not yet on the national grid.
Reliable, affordable energy is our commitment to every Ghanaian household and
business.
