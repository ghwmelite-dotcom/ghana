## What we do

The Ministry of Food and Agriculture feeds Ghana and grows its economy.
We provide extension officers who help farmers adopt better practices,
manage the national strategic grain reserve, and run irrigation schemes
to reduce dependence on rain-fed farming. We regulate agricultural inputs
so farmers get genuine seeds and fertilisers. Our goal is a food-secure,
commercially driven farming sector where every farmer earns a fair income.
