## What we do

The Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection champions the rights of
women, children, and vulnerable people in Ghana. The LEAP programme delivers
cash transfers to extremely poor households so children stay in school and
families meet basic needs. The Department of Social Welfare supports people with
disabilities, the elderly, and orphans. DOVVSU works alongside the Police to
protect survivors of domestic violence. We drive policies to close the gender
gap in education, employment, and public life so every Ghanaian — regardless of
gender or circumstance — can thrive.
