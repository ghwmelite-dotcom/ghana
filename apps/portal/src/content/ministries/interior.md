## What we do

The Ministry of the Interior keeps communities safe and manages who enters
and stays in Ghana. We oversee the National Identification Authority, which
issues the Ghana Card — the national digital identity for every citizen.
The Ghana Police Service, Ghana Immigration Service, and National Fire Service
all report to us. We also coordinate disaster response through NADMO, helping
communities recover from floods, fires, and other emergencies.
