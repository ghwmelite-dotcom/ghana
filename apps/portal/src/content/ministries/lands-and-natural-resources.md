## What we do

The Ministry of Lands and Natural Resources protects Ghana's most valuable
physical assets. The Lands Commission processes land title registration so your
property rights are secure. The Minerals Commission licenses mining operations
and ensures communities benefit. The Forestry Commission manages Ghana's forests
to balance timber revenue with conservation. We are tackling illegal mining
(galamsey) and illegal logging to preserve the environment for future
generations.
