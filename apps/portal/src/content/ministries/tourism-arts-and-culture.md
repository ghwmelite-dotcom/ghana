## What we do

The Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture showcases the best of Ghana to the
world. The Ghana Tourism Authority promotes destinations from the slave castles
of the Central Region to the waterfalls of the Volta. The Ghana Museums and
Monuments Board preserves our cultural sites for future generations. The
National Commission on Culture protects and promotes Ghanaian traditions,
including Adinkra symbols, Kente weaving, and our diverse festivals. We
invest in the creative arts so Ghanaian musicians, filmmakers, and
designers can compete globally.
