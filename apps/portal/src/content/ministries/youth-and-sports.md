## What we do

The Ministry of Youth and Sports invests in the people who will build
Ghana's future. The Youth Employment Agency places young Ghanaians in jobs
and training programmes across the country. The National Sports Authority
manages stadia, sports facilities, and supports national sporting teams —
from the Black Stars to Para-athletics. The National Youth Council engages
young people in civic life. We believe sport builds character, health,
and national pride — and we invest accordingly.
