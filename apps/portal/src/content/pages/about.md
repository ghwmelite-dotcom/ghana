## What this portal is

gov.gh is the national government portal of the Republic of Ghana. It is built and maintained by the Office of the Head of Civil Service (OHCS) under the digital transformation mandate of the Government of Ghana.

You can use this portal to:

- find and complete government services online
- read official news and announcements from ministries
- locate contact details for every government ministry and department
- access public data, publications, and policy documents

## Who runs it

OHCS leads the portal on behalf of the Government of Ghana. Day-to-day operations, content, and technical delivery are managed by the OHCS Digital Directorate. Each ministry is responsible for the accuracy of its own content.

## Open by design

The code that powers this portal is published as open source under the MIT licence. All written content is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC-BY 4.0) unless otherwise stated. You are free to share, adapt, and build on it, provided you credit the Government of Ghana.

The Ghana Design System, which defines the visual and interaction standards used here, is published at [design.gov.gh](https://design.gov.gh) and available to all government institutions and the public.

## Commitment to citizens

This portal is built to the highest standards of accessibility, performance, and plain language. Every service must work for a citizen on a basic smartphone over a slow mobile connection. If something does not work for you, please [contact us](/contact).
