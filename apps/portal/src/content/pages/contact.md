## Portal feedback

We want to hear from you. If something on this portal is wrong, unclear, or not working, email us at [feedback@gov.gh](mailto:feedback@gov.gh). We read every message and respond within five working days.

## Ministry and service enquiries

For questions about a specific government service or ministry, go to the [Ministries directory](/ministries) and contact the relevant ministry directly. Portal staff cannot answer questions on behalf of individual ministries.

## Accessibility issues

If you find an accessibility barrier on any page, email [accessibility@gov.gh](mailto:accessibility@gov.gh). Please include the page address and a description of the problem. See our [Accessibility statement](/accessibility) for more details.

## Media enquiries

Journalists seeking comment from the Office of the Head of Civil Service should email the OHCS Communications Unit at [communications@ohcs.gov.gh](mailto:communications@ohcs.gov.gh). For enquiries about specific ministries, contact the ministry's public relations unit directly.

## Right to Information (RTI)

Ghana's Right to Information Act 2019 (Act 989) gives you the right to request information held by public institutions. Submit an RTI request by emailing [rti@information.gov.gh](mailto:rti@information.gov.gh).

## Anti-Corruption Hotline

To report corruption involving any public official or government institution, call the Commission on Human Rights and Administrative Justice (CHRAJ) Anti-Corruption Hotline:

**0800 100 280** — free of charge, available 24 hours, 7 days a week. You may report anonymously.
