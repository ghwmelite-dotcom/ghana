## Before you apply

Have a licensed solicitor prepare the deed poll or statutory declaration
before visiting the Registrar-General's Department. The document must state
your old name, your new name, and the reason for the change.

## Step by step

1. Instruct a solicitor to prepare your deed poll or statutory declaration.
2. Have the document sworn before a Commissioner for Oaths or a notary.
3. Visit the Registrar-General's Department with the deed, your Ghana Card,
   birth certificate, and two passport photographs.
4. Complete the application form and pay the registration fee.
5. Receive your certificate of change of name within 5 working days.

## After you apply

Use your certificate to update your Ghana Card at any NIA centre, and your
passport through the Ghana Immigration Service. Notify your employer, bank,
and other institutions of the change.
