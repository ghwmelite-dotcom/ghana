## Before you travel

Check the Ghana Immigration Service website or your nearest Ghanaian
embassy to confirm your country is on the visa-on-arrival list. Obtain your
yellow fever vaccination certificate — you will not be admitted to Ghana
without it.

## Step by step

1. On arrival at Kotoka International Airport, proceed to the Ghana
   Immigration Service visa-on-arrival counter before the immigration
   passport control queues.
2. Present your passport, return ticket, accommodation proof, and yellow
   fever certificate.
3. Pay the visa fee (US dollars are accepted; check the current rate at
   gis.gov.gh before travelling).
4. Receive your visa sticker and proceed through passport control.
5. Customs clearance follows as normal.

## After you arrive

Your visa on arrival is valid for 30 days from the date of entry. If you
need to stay longer, apply for a visa extension at the GIS headquarters
before your visa expires. Overstaying attracts fines and may affect future
travel to Ghana.
