## Before you pay

Log in to the SSNIT Member Self-Service portal to confirm your current balance
and check that previous contributions are correctly posted. If you are an
employer, use the Employer Self-Service portal.

## Step by step

1. Log in at ssnit.org.gh with your SSNIT member number and PIN.
2. Select "Make a Contribution" from the self-employed or voluntary menu.
3. Enter the contribution amount and select your payment channel (MoMo, bank, or
   GhIPSS).
4. Complete the payment and save the transaction receipt.
5. Verify the credit appears in your contribution history within 3 working days.

## After you pay

Your contribution history determines your pension entitlement. Check your
statement at least once a year. Report any missing credits to your nearest SSNIT
office immediately.
