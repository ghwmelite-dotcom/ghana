## Before you apply

Ensure your business has a TIN and all employees have their Ghana Card PINs
ready (which serve as their individual TINs). You will need to set up a payroll
system that calculates PAYE using the current GRA income tax bands.

## Step by step

1. Log in to the GRA Taxpayer Portal at gra.gov.gh with your business TIN.
2. Select "Employer Registration" under the PAYE section.
3. Enter your business details and the number of employees.
4. Submit the form and receive your Employer PAYE Reference Number.
5. Set up monthly PAYE deductions from employee salaries.
6. Remit the collected PAYE to GRA on or before the 15th of each month.

## After you register

File monthly PAYE returns even in months when no salaries are paid. Issue each
employee an annual P9A payslip summary. Keep payroll records for at least 6
years in case of audit.
