## Before you apply

Obtain a site licence for your manufacturing, import, or distribution premises
before submitting a product application. A complete and accurate dossier reduces
review time significantly — check the FDA's product-specific guidelines on
fdaghana.gov.gh before compiling your documents.

## Step by step

1. Create an account on the FDA Product Registration Portal at fdaghana.gov.gh.
2. Select the correct product category (food, medicine, cosmetic, medical
   device, or household chemical).
3. Complete the application form and upload all required documents and
   laboratory certificates.
4. Pay the application fee through the FDA portal.
5. The FDA reviews your dossier and may request additional information.
6. Once approved, download your product registration certificate and use the
   assigned registration number on all product labels.

## After you register

Product registrations are valid for 5 years (medicines) or as specified on your
certificate. Renew before expiry. Notify the FDA immediately if you change the
product formula, label, or source manufacturer.
