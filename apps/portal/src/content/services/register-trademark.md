## Before you apply

Conduct a trademark search on the RGD database to check that your mark
does not conflict with an existing registration. Consider working with a
registered trademark agent for complex applications.

## Step by step

1. Conduct a trademark search at the Registrar-General's Department online
   or in person.
2. Prepare the representation of your mark and the list of goods or services
   by Nice Classification class.
3. Submit TM Form 1 (Application for Registration of Trade Mark) with all
   supporting documents.
4. Pay the application fee per class.
5. The RGD examines your application. If accepted, it is published in the
   Trademarks Journal for a 2-month opposition period.
6. If no opposition is received, the certificate of registration is issued.

## After you register

Renew your trademark every 10 years before expiry. Use the ® symbol only
after registration is confirmed. Monitor the Trademarks Journal for
conflicting marks and file an opposition promptly if needed.
